Additional Travel Tips

Make the most of your visit with these helpful suggestions

 Photography Tips
  • Bring extra batteries (limited charging facilities)
  • Best light for bird photography: early morning
  • Respect local customs when photographing people
  • Waterproof your equipment during rainy season
 Environmental Ethics
  • Follow Leave No Trace principles
  • Stay on designated trails
  • Don't disturb wildlife or their habitats
  • Support local conservation efforts
