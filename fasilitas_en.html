Facility Information

Klabili Tourism Village provides various modern and traditional facilities to ensure comfort and satisfaction for every visiting tourist.

Free WiFi

Free internet access throughout the tourism village area for easy communication.

Dining Area

Dining area with local food menu and clean, comfortable facilities.

Parking Area

Spacious and safe parking area for both motorcycles and cars.

Public Restroom

Clean and well-maintained public restroom facilities with high hygiene standards.

First Aid

First aid facilities for tourist safety and emergency situations.

Souvenir Shop

Shop selling various village specialties and local handicraft products.

Information Center

Tourism information center with complete guides and tourist location maps.

Church

Comfortable prayer place for tourists who want to worship.

🌟 Klabili Village Facilities

Complete facilities for tourist comfort

✨ Electricity & Clean Water

All houses are connected to electricity and clean water, making tourists feel secure and comfortable during their stay in the village.

📶 Signal & Internet

Even in the remote area, Klabili has signal and internet access. So you can still update your stories or video call your family.

🏡 Comfortable Homestay

Stay with local residents in simple but warm homestays. Facilities include beds, mosquito nets, and toilets to ensure comfortable rest.

🩺 Nearest Health Center

Need health services? Don't worry, there's a health center (Puskesmas) in the district capital ready to help tourists and local residents.

🛍 Village Shops & BUMDes

Daily shopping made easy. There are village shops and BUMDes (Village-Owned Enterprises) providing snacks to basic necessities—shop while supporting the local economy.

🌿 Birdwatching Rest House

During birdwatching or trekking, tourists can rest or take shelter in comfortable rest houses in the middle of the tropical forest.

♻ Village Cleanliness Program

Every Friday, Klabili residents hold "Clean Friday" with plastic waste bins in every house. The village stays pristine, making tourists feel more at home. This program demonstrates the village's commitment to environmental preservation and providing a clean and comfortable tourism experience.

Klabili Tourism Village offers authentic tourism experiences with natural beauty, local culture, and the warmth of the Moi Tribe community.
