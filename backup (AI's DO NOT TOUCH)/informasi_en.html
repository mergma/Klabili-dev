How to Reach Klabili

From Sorong

Flight to Domine Eduard Osok Airport

Then a road transfer to Klabili via Sausapor (~2 hours)

From Sorong

Route: Sorong → Klabili

4×4 vehicles or trail motorcycles (~2 hours)

By Sea

Nearest port: Sausapor (weekly cargo)

Speedboat Sorong–Sausapor (~2 hours) is planned

Photography Tips
  • Best golden hours: 06:00-07:00 and 17:00-18:00
  • Bring a telephoto lens for bird photos
  • Prepare camera protection from rain
  • Respect the privacy of local residents
Environmental Etiquette
  • Do not leave trash in nature
  • Use established trails
  • Do not pick plants or disturb wildlife
  • Support local and eco-friendly products
