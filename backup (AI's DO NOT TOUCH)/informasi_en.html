Village Address

Klabili Village
Selemkai District
Tambrauw Regency
Southwest Papua Province
Indonesia

Located at an altitude of 800 meters above sea level

From Sorong: Flight to Domine Eduard Osok Airport, then overland to Klabili via Sausapor (~4 hours).

From Manokwari: Flight to Rendani Airport, then Susi Air to Werur Airport, followed by overland to Klabili.

From Sorong: Route Sorong → Sausapor → Klabili by 4×4 vehicle or trail motorcycle (~2–4 hours).

From Manokwari: Route Manokwari → Sorong → Klabili (~8–10 hours).

No direct ferry service. Nearest port is Sausapor (weekly cargo), speedboat Sorong–Sausapor (~2 hours) is in planning.

Travel Tips & Information

Important information for your visit

Weather & Climate

Cool tropical climate with temperatures ranging from 18-25°C. Rainy season: November-March. Best visiting time: April-October.

Travel Tips

What to Bring: Warm clothes, rain gear, comfortable hiking shoes, insect repellent
Best Time: Early morning for birdwatching, afternoon for cultural activities
Duration: Minimum 2-3 days to fully experience the village
