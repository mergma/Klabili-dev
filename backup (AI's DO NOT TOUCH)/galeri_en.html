Exotic bird diversity inhabiting Papua's tropical forests

Bird of Paradise

The iconic bird of Papua with stunning beautiful feathers and captivating colors.

Cassowary

Large flightless bird endemic to Papua with distinctive bone helmet on its head.

Victoria Crowned Pigeon

Large pigeon with elegant feather crown, one of the most beautiful birds in Papua.

Knobbed Hornbill

Large hornbill with distinctive knob on its beak, very important in forest ecosystem.

Blue-black Kingfisher

Stunning kingfisher with blue-black colors, often seen around rivers and streams.
