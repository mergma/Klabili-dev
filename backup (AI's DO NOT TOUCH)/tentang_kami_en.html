Klabili Village Profile

A hidden gem in the highlands of West Papua

800 masl

Stunning Natural Beauty

Klabili Village is located in Selemkay District, Tambrauw Regency, Southwest Papua, on a highland surrounded by green hills and clear rivers. At an altitude of 800 meters above sea level, this village offers cool and fresh air with a comfortable tropical climate throughout the year. The stunning natural scenery and pristine environment make Klabili a special tourism destination.

Moi Tribe Cultural Heritage

Klabili Village is home to the Moi Tribe community who have inhabited this area for generations. The preserved local wisdom and traditions are the main attractions for tourists who want to experience authentic cultural experiences.

Vision & Mission

Our commitment to sustainable tourism village development

Vision

To make Klabili Village a sustainable cultural and natural tourism destination, while preserving the local wisdom of the Moi Tribe and the natural beauty of West Papua.

Mission

  • Develop natural and cultural tourism potential sustainably
  • Empower local communities through the tourism sector
  • Preserve the culture and traditions of the Moi Tribe
  • Maintain environmental conservation and biodiversity
