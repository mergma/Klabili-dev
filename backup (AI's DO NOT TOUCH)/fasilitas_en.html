Main Facilities

 

Facility Information

Klabili Tourism Village provides various modern and traditional facilities to ensure comfort and satisfaction for every visiting tourist.

Electricity & Clean Water

All houses are connected to electricity and clean water for visitor comfort.

Signal & Internet

Signal and internet access are available so visitors stay connected.

Comfortable Homestays

Homestays managed by the community with clean basic facilities and friendly service.

Nearest Health Center

Access to health services is available at the health center in the nearest sub-district town.

Village Shops & BUMDes

Local kiosks and BUMDes provide daily needs and local products.

Birdwatching Rest House

Rest houses for birdwatchers and trekking participants inside the forest area.

Village Cleanliness Program

Periodic cleanliness initiatives keep the village tidy and comfortable for visitors.

Church

A place of worship for the local community and visiting tourists.

Additional Facilities

Supporting facilities for a more complete tourism experience

Photo Spots

Various interesting photo spots with beautiful natural scenery backgrounds that are Instagram-worthy.

Trekking Trails

Safe trekking trails with local guides to explore the natural beauty around the village.

Camping Areas

Designated camping areas with necessary facilities for an enjoyable outdoor experience.

Klabili Tourism Village offers authentic tourism experiences with natural beauty, local culture, and the warmth of the Moi Tribe community.
